My name is María Díaz de Léon Derby and I am a PhD student in the joint UC Berkeley - UCSF Bioengineering Department where I am advised by Prof. Dan Fletcher. Before Berkeley, I completed a bachelor in Mechatronics Engineering at the Tecnológico de Monterrey (ITESM) in Mexico.

I am interested in developing machine learning techniques for the diagnosis of Neglected Tropical Diseases, a group of conditions that affect one billion of the world's most vulnerable people.

Here is my CV. You can reach me at maria.diaz@berkeley.edu.
